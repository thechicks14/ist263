Background

Steve Angeli is a talented quarterback with solid mechanics and a strong arm, currently playing for Syracuse University. Known for his leadership and poise under pressure, Angeli took over the starting role and quickly became the anchor of the offense. Originally from Westfield, New Jersey, he was a highly-rated recruit who has steadily developed his skill set within the collegiate system.

His 2024 season saw significant statistical success, demonstrating his ability to manage games effectively and deliver accurate passes downfield. He possesses the necessary size and intelligence to command an NFL offense, making him an intriguing prospect for the 2026 Draft.

Scouting Report

Overall Grade: 82/100

Strengths

  • Above-average arm strength and velocity.
  • Good pocket presence and ability to step up into pressure.
  • High football IQ and pre-snap processing.
  • Strong leadership qualities.
  • Accurate on intermediate throws.

Areas for Development

  • Footwork can become inconsistent under heavy duress.
  • Needs to improve anticipation on deep throws.
  • Mobility is adequate, but not a strength.
  • Must limit turnovers against NFL-caliber defenses.

Scout's Analysis

Angeli projects as a reliable pocket passer with the mental makeup and physical tools to develop into a long-term backup or potential starter in the NFL. His best assets are his toughness and accuracy on timing routes. While he may not possess the elite athleticism of some top prospects, his consistent production and work ethic make him a valuable mid-round target who could outperform his draft stock.
